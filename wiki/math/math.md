# 🧮 Calculator & Solvers

Mathematical utilities: complex expressions parsing, primes, prime factors, and Fibonacci generation.

---

> [!NOTE]
> **Category Aliases**: `calc`

## 🧩 Subcommands List

| Command | Title | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [`evaluate`](./evaluate.md) | Arithmetic Expression Evaluator | Evaluates complex algebraic/arithmetic expressions (+, -, *, /, %, ^, sin, cos, tan, log) in the terminal. |
| [`prime`](./prime.md) | Number Tester | Checks primality status, perfect number flags, and Fibonacci sequence membership of a number. |
| [`fibonacci`](./fibonacci.md) | Fibonacci Generator | Generates a list containing the first N items in the Fibonacci sequence. |
| [`factor`](./factor.md) | Prime Factor Solver | Resolves positive integers into their prime factor products. |

---

## 🚀 How to Execute

To call any feature under this module, prepend the category name `math` (or one of its aliases):

```bash
nhash math [subcommand] [arguments] [options]
```
